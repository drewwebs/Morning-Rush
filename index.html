Shinigami Cafe
Welcome to the Shinigami Cafe!
Ryuk the Japanese death god has become bored, and decided to run a coffee shop to pass the time. Help him serve all of the guests before they get too impatient.
Press "enter" to start serving!

Game Controls
  • Stand in front of an item and press "Space" to pick it up
  • Combine items to create drinks
  • Serve an item by walking to the pickup area, and pressing key corresponding to the items slot (1-3)
  • Serve a guest before they run out of patience and walk out!
